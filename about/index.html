I have always been interested in the ways human behaviour and interaction shapes our built environment. In recent years, I have increasingly dedicated myself to quantitative methods in the field of simulation and other data science methods. I am experienced with various programming language and tools, transport modelling solutions and geo-spatial software. However, my most preferend language is R. I also like making clear and attractive visualisation using Adobe CS.
